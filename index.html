!Games, Art!

Blending immersion and story

Many video games have transcended their banal gamehood, all to commonly found near the inception of gaming, and have become vibrant microcosms of one's imagination.

Ambiance is now felt through a fusion of visual affects and music, that bring your heart closer to the story at hand. Art may be subjective, but many games are considered masterpieces in their own rights.

Games have evolved

Video game design now considers many aspects of the player's experience, from thrills to heartbreak.
